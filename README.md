# Ford Strategy Analysis Dashboard

An interactive dashboard showcasing Ford Motor Company's strategic transformation from 2020 to 2025, built as part of DSCI-5330 Business Analytics & Intelligence at the University of North Texas.

## Features

### Executive Summary
- Real-time KPI overview with revenue, EBIT, and employee metrics
- Business unit comparison radar chart
- Interactive sparkline trends

### Business Unit Deep Dives
- **Ford Blue**: Traditional ICE vehicles - cost leadership strategy
- **Model e**: Electric vehicles - strategic market penetration
- **Ford Pro**: Commercial solutions - differentiation through software/services

### Framework Explorer
Explore 12 course frameworks applied to Ford's strategic context:
- Porter's Five Forces
- Birkinshaw & Goddard Management Model
- MIT Decision Category Framework
- STP Framework (Segmentation-Targeting-Positioning)
- Consumer Behavior & Decision-Making
- Focused Factory Concept (Skinner)
- Resource-Based View
- Strategy Implementation Roadmap (SIR)
- Time Value of Money & NPV
- And more...

### Transformation Story
An 8-step guided narrative walking through Ford's journey:
1. The Challenge (2020)
2. New Leadership Vision
3. Restructuring into business units
4. Financial Recovery
5. Marketing Evolution
6. Operations Transformation
7. Strategic Tensions
8. Looking Forward

## Tech Stack

- **React 18** with Vite
- **Tailwind CSS v4** for styling
- **Recharts** for data visualization
- **React Router** for navigation
- **Lucide React** for icons

## Data Sources

- Ford 10-K Annual Reports (2020-2024)
- Ford Annual Shareholder Reports
- Course readings from DSCI-5330
- Industry benchmarks from J.D. Power, Statista
